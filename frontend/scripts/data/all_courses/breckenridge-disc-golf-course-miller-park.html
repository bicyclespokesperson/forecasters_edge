Course Details

Target Type: DISCatcher

Hole Length: Under 300ft: 16 | 300 - 400ft: 2 | Over 400ft: 0

Description

Terrain changes throughout with uphill and downhill shots. Two sets of tees for each basket.

Directions

From Dallas; I-20 - exit 414. Merge onto US-180 W and go 75.3 miles to a left on Old Caddo Ave. Parking lot on the left. 1st tee at the top of the hill behind the pool.
