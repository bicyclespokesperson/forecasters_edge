Forecaster's Edge


This site displays nearby disc golf courses and sorts them by the best weather conditions. Useful for deciding on a course to play later in the day.

Location Input

Enter either a ZIP Code (example: 12345) or a latitude, longitude coordinate pair (example: 12.345, -123.456).

Weather Score

The weather score goes from 1 to 10, with 1 representing terrible weather and 10 representing perfect weather. It roughly corresponds to the inverse of the chance of precipitation; e.g. a course with a 70% chance of rain will have a score of 3. The score is lowered if courses are too windy, too hot, or too cold.

Courses

This site includes courses located in the USA that have at least 18 holes.

Timezones

The time selected is assumed to be in the course's local timezone. This means the weather scores shown in the table aren't guaranteed to be at the same time if the table includes courses in multiple timezones.
